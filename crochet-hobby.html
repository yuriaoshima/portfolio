A Little Bit About Crochet

Crochet is a craft similar to knitting. However, unlike knitting, crochet is done with a crochet hook instead of two knitting needles. Crochet is also much easier to learn and is a good start for someone who is interested in making simple scarf designs, blanket, headbands, and much more!

To get started in crochet is simple. There are only three basic steps you need to know

There are three fundamental basic stitches in crochet. These are the single, double, and triple crochet stitches. A crochet product is made with one or more of these stitch types. There are also more advanced crochet stitches for the fancy patterns. However these are more complicated and must be learned after mastering the three fundamental stitches!
